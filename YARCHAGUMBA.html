AGENCY- ONE47

Enticing people with a fungus story,
Is what brought this brand its glory.
From nature’s grace to crafted art,
Yarchagumba holds Nepal at heart.

Yarchagumba is a luxury wellness brand from Nepal, known for premium products like whiskey and shilajit.

CHALLENGE

A heritage-rich name with low cultural recall in a category crowded with generic luxury cues

INSIGHT

Global wellness consumers increasingly seek authentic origin stories, not anonymous premium branding.

IDEA

Position Yarchagumba as a Himalayan cultural artefact, not just a luxury product.

EXECUTION

Built the identity around the discovery legend of Yartsa Gunbu, using illustration-led storytelling and Nepali cultural motifs to systemise the brand across touchpoints.
