Jumpp

Concept Ideation
Social Media Illustrations
AGENCY- ONE47

From carousel crafted to teach with ease,
To gaming reels designed to engage and please.
Where money does more,
The promise held tight,
Turning finance into something light.

Jumpp is a fintech startup that empowers people in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities to manage their finances, save, invest, insure, and budget with help from their AI assistant, jAI.

CHALLENGE

Their visual identity relied on expressionless vector illustrations, great for branding, but difficult to make emotionally engaging. We had only six illustrations to start with.

EXECUTION

We developed over 100+ illustrations in six months, creating an accessible and vibrant visual system for the brand. Our reels consistently gained traction—and we grew the account from 0 to 6K followers.
Live at @lets.jumpp
